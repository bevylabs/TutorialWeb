Tutorial 1 : Self Watering Plant

STEP 1

Firstly you can find out an empty plastic bottle

STEP 2

Cut down it smoothly as shown in the picture.

STEP 3

Get the head side of the bottle and create a hole through the cork

STEP 4

Add smooth string through the hole and after that add some pebbles on the bottom

STEP 5

Touch the string and the plant root acording to the figure

STEP 6

Add some soil/fertilizers into the bottom of bottle

STEP 7

Fill the bottle using soil acording to the figure

STEP 8

Add some pebbles for covering the soil area and Attach the bottom side part as cutting previously (2 STEP).

STEP 9

Fill some amount of water to the bottom side cutted part.


Tutorial 2 : Refreshing a Wilted Plant

Hi

Has your favorite plant has lost its freshness and seems to be wilted? Then here we have a solution for you. Let proceed with it.

STEP 1

Add a table spoon of coffee to a blender.

STEP 2

Then add a banana skin to the blender.

STEP 3

Finally add 2 egg shells to the above combination.

STEP 4

Grind them finely.

STEP 5

Add a table spoon of the above mixture near the root of the plant.

STEP 6

Leave it for few hours Your will get back your lovely refreshed plant.
